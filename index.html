Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is my data?

We use bank-level security and end-to-end encryption to protect your data. We are fully GDPR compliant and never share your data without your explicit consent. Security is our top priority.

What integrations do you support?

We support a wide range of accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero, Sage), banking institutions, payment processors (Stripe, PayPal), and marketing platforms. We are constantly adding new integrations based on user feedback.

Can I upgrade/downgrade anytime?

Yes, absolutely. You can change your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes will be prorated and applied to your next billing cycle. No questions asked.

Do you offer onboarding support?

We offer comprehensive onboarding support for all plans. This includes detailed documentation, video tutorials, and dedicated email support. Our Growth and Advanced plans also include personalized onboarding calls to ensure you get the most out of the platform.
